Privacy Policy for Time in 15 Minutes Chrome Extension

Last updated: 05/06/2023

This privacy policy governs your use of the "Time in 15 Minutes" Chrome extension. Please read this policy carefully before using the extension.

What information do we collect?

We do not collect any personal or sensitive user data with the "Time in 15 Minutes" Chrome extension. The extension calculates the time in 15 minutes from the user's current time locally on the user's device and does not store or transmit any data.
